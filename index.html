The Story

The Sicilian black bee is one of the island's most remarkable natural treasures. Perfectly adapted to the unique ecosystem surrounding Mount Etna, it plays a vital role in biodiversity and environmental health.

Black Bees Etna was created to help protect this extraordinary species while building sustainable economic opportunities for local communities.

What We're Building

  • Premium artisanal honey and hive products
  • Natural skincare and wellness products
  • Sustainable candles and gift collections
  • Adopt-a-Hive conservation experiences
  • Biodiversity restoration initiatives
  • Educational and environmental programs
  • Partnerships with local producers and artisans
